Pro Travel Tips

✈️

Book Mid-Week

Flights are significantly cheaper when you fly on Tuesdays or Wednesdays. Avoid weekend departures to save up to 40%.

🎒

Roll, Don't Fold

Rolling your clothes saves massive space and prevents wrinkles. Use packing cubes to organize by category.

🍱

Eat Off-Main-Street

Walk two blocks away from tourist attractions. The food will be more authentic and half the price.

🕵️

Incognito Browsing

Airlines track your cookies. Always search for flights in "Incognito Mode" to avoid price hikes based on your search history.

🗺️

Offline Maps

Download Google Maps for your destination while you have WiFi. It works via GPS even without mobile data!

💳

Local Currency

Always pay in the local currency when using a card. Never let the ATM or terminal do the conversion for you.
